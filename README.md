# Extempore Activity – IT in Automobile

## Course
Critical Thinking, Design Thinking, Leadership and Teamwork

## Task
Task 08 – Extempore Activity

## Topic
IT in Automobile

## Objective
The objective of this activity is to understand the impact of Information Technology on the automobile industry and how modern technologies are improving transportation systems.

## Introduction
Information Technology (IT) has transformed the automobile industry by making vehicles smarter, safer, and more efficient. Modern vehicles use software, sensors, artificial intelligence, and communication technologies to improve performance and enhance the driving experience.

## Key Applications of IT in Automobile
- GPS Navigation Systems
- Smart Vehicle Connectivity
- Driver Assistance Systems
- Automatic Parking Systems
- Vehicle Diagnostics and Monitoring
- Predictive Maintenance

## Benefits
- Improved Road Safety
- Better Fuel Efficiency
- Real-Time Navigation
- Enhanced User Experience
- Reduced Maintenance Costs

## Challenges
- Cybersecurity Risks
- Data Privacy Issues
- High Development Costs
- Dependence on Software Systems

## Conclusion
Information Technology is revolutionizing the automobile industry by enabling innovation, automation, and intelligent transportation. Future vehicles will become more connected, autonomous, and efficient through continuous technological advancements.
